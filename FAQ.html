General

Q: When can I join the course?
A: You may join the course any time! Give us a call and we can get you registered in the course immediately, and schedule driving lessons for a time that works.

Q: Can I take a training course even though I have never driven before?
A: Absolutely! We are here to help and a large portion of our students have never driven before!

Q: Do I need a class 7 License before taking driving lessons?
A: Yes. By law you are required to have a class 7 license and a class 5 non-GDL driver next to you before you can operate a vehicle.

Q: Can I begin the training without a class 7 License?
A: Yes! You may begin the online course/classroom portion of the learning, however to operate any vehicle you are required to have a class 7 license.


Certificate of Completion

Q: What is the benefit of a certificate of completion?
A: The certificate of completion is a document which you can provide to your insurance company to reduce your insurance costs up to 40%.

Q: What is required to get a certificate of completion?
A: There are two types of certificates of completion. Firstly for those who are class 7, they must complete the course based portion and if they achieve over 80%, they obtain the certificate. For those who are attempting class 5 licensing, they are required to achieve over 75% on the driving skill evaluation as well as 80% on the course based portion to obtain the certificate.
